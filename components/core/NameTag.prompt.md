**NameTag** — the soft tinted pill that identifies a person or carries a small label.

```jsx
<NameTag person="junghwa">정화</NameTag>
<NameTag person="haein">해인</NameTag>
<NameTag tone="neutral">주말</NameTag>
```

`person` picks purple (정화) or pink (해인); `tone` overrides with `purple` / `pink` / `neutral`.
