**VitaminCheck** — a single vitamin checkbox row inside a calendar cell; the label strikes through in the person's hue when checked.

```jsx
<VitaminCheck person="junghwa" label="정화 비타민" checked />
<VitaminCheck person="haein" label="해인 비타민" missed />
```

`person` sets the accent color, `checked` strikes the label, `missed` shows the pink dot for past unchecked days.
